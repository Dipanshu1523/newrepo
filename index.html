Biography

Ratan Tata, born on December 28, 1937, is an Indian industrialist and philanthropist.
He was the chairman of Tata Sons and played a key role in transforming Tata Group into a global brand. Throughout his life, Tata invested in over 40 start-ups,
primarily in a personal capacity, with additional investments through his firm, RNT Capital Advisors.

Ratan Tata's Legacy

Leader's Mindset

Ratan Tata's legacy as a leader, philanthropist, and visionary continues to inspire people worldwide. Known for his ethical leadership and commitment to social causes, he transformed the Tata Group into a global powerhouse while prioritising integrity and responsibility. Tata's innovative ventures, such as the Tata Nano and acquisitions of Jaguar Land Rover, demonstrate his courage to take risks with a long-term vision. Tata’s dedication to both business excellence and social welfare, through initiatives like Tata Trusts, reinforces the idea that success must serve the greater good. Ratan tata once said:

“I don’t believe in taking the right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right.”

- Ratan Tata
